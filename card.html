Turtle

Turtles belong to one of the oldest reptile groups in the world beating snakes, crocodiles and alligators!

  • Scientific Name:Chelonia mydas.
  • Average Length: 139 centimeters.
  • Average Lifespan: 40 years.
  • Habitat: Tropical and subtropical waters.

Turtles are diapsids of the order Testudines characterized by a special bony developed from their ribs and acting as a shield. "Turtle" may refer to the order as a whole or to fresh-water and sea-dwelling testudines. The order Testudines includes both extant and extinct species.
